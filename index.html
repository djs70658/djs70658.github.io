My Dream

Traveling has always been something that I've dreamt of doing. Unfortunately, life always seems to get in the way. Whether it be financial issues, time restraints, or personal obstacles; I always find myself with some reason as to why I can't travel the world. I know that I should try to be spontaneous every now and then, but I'm a dreamer. I'm a dreamer that likes to plan. Boy, have I been planning.

While I wait for the right moment, whether it be tomorrow or next year, I placate myself by dreaming of all the places I will go. I want to travel to places that I can't even imagine existing. I want to explore mountains and swin in lakes. I want to experience life outside of little old Georgia. I want to be a part of the beautiful world out there. There is such a vast and magnificent world out there just waiting for me to come explore it, and I intend to face this adventure head on with a reserve of dreams to fulfill.

I made this website with the intention of using it to compile annecdotes about my future travels, and eventually I will use it as a portfolio of the places I have gone. I have pieced together links, lists, tables, and much more to help me get a better look at the future I have laid out before my feet. While I sit and continue with my studies, I know that this will always be a place for me to sit and remind myself that there is so much more than Georgia. Journaling has always been a hobby of mine, and I see this web page as a virtual extension of my journaling. I know that this will easily be one of my favorite pages to visit.

Not Sure Where to Start

I want to travel, but where would I even start? I have no idea where I might begin this journey outside of my comfort zone. It makes me nervous and excited at the same time. I have to pick just the right place to kick start this journey. Traveling, alone especially, is a scary thing. You must learn to embrace your fears and take the first step to make dreams come true.

The Choice is Mine

When it comes down to it, I know that it will really be up to me to decide where and when to start this amazing journey around the world. I will have to carefully weigh my options and make a choice that is right for me. I know this, but I can still take a moment to see what everyone else thinks!
